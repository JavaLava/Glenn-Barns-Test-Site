Operation & Tools

The mission is to deliver powerful, accessible and cost effective operational improvements, and advance the performance of you and your organisation.

It can be part of an operation or organisation, or a section which is cause for concern.

Operational improvement actions produce significant direct and indirect gains. Along with gains via tapping into the“hidden factory”.

The hidden factory might be 15% to 40% of total company effort. The notion of the hidden factory is bound up with the metric COPQ (cost of poor quality). It includes rework customer dissatisfaction, excess stock waste, cycle time, uptime.

Operational efficiency improvement drives customer satisfaction.

The reduction of process variability increases quality and reduces scrap and increase customer satisfaction. Capable processes enable less scrap and rework, require less material inputs, consume less energy, and yield improved administration. Noise is removed from the system which enables you to best utilise your time.


  • Lean

  • 6 Sigma DMAIC approach either Business or Technical/Engineering

  • Kaizen events

  • COPQ (Cost of poor quality)

  • Waste reduction

  • Dock to Dock time. (Cycle time)

Instilling methodology and sometimes technology to insure process and organisational improvements.

All the above can be actioned and costed to suit your requirements. In some cases we are confident that following a survey we can work on a percent saved basis, thereby costing no upfront charges.
